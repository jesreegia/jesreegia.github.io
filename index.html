Insurance Tasks and Responsibilities I’ve Handled

  • Quoting

    • Homeowners (Home / Condo / Renters), Dwelling Fire, Auto, Umbrella and Jewelry using Rating Software such as Quote Rush and PL Rater, as well as various carrier websites for accurate and efficient rate comparison and policy processing.

  • Endorsement

    • Mortgagee Change in Carrier websites and AMS

    • Removal of lienholder

    • Adding/Removing Vehicles or Drivers

    • Adjusting the Deductibles

  • Invoice / Payments

    • Processing of Payments via Credit card

    • Monitoring of Payment through different carrier websites

    • Sending follow up emails and texts to insureds using Agency Zoom

  • Cancellation

    • Generating Cancellation Request / Policy Release and sending to insured/s for e-signature

    • Cancelling the policy thru carrier website

    • Sending the signed cancellation form thru email

  • Servicing Tasks

    • Recording Commissions in AMS

    • Electronic Documentation of Claims, Signed Documents and New Business Sheets

    • Coordinating with carrier underwriting via websites or phone to secure policy updates, approvals, or clarifications.

  • Certificate / Policy Issuance

    • Obtaining ID Cards and sending to insured

    • Issuing COI and sending to insured/s and certificate holder/s

    • Ensuring Policy Accuracy through Policy Quality Check before issuing the policy to insured and/or Certificate Holders.
